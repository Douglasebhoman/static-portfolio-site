ABOUT

The writer behind the work

I write literary fiction and personal essays—quiet stories about complicated people. My work explores the spaces between words, the unspoken tensions that shape our relationships.

Over the last decade, I've worked with editors at Penguin Random House, Tin House, and The Paris Review. My stories have been translated into twelve languages and adapted for the screen.

I also coach writers: from first chapters to final drafts, with a focus on voice and structure. Whether you're crafting a novel or refining an essay, I help you find the heart of your story.
